The Burning Tower

Note to self: do not build your tower out of Wood³.

Description: A tower located on Basamba that is forever burning, due to the fact it was made purely out of Wood³. This was meant to stabilize the structure, but obviously backfired when in 1963, someone tripped and fell while holding a candle. Everyone that was in the tower burnt up and became some sort of fire being. They later formed a faction called Flare9. The tower’s roof has collapsed but they still reside as they have burnt the surrounding field to claim as their own. Currently, 16 spirits reside in the tower and the surrounding area.
