Evan Lobotomy

Evan, you’re too normal. Here’s chaos

Description: Evan is the definition of normal. He also has a can-do-will-most-likely-not-do type of attitude towards most things and often finds himself in really stupid/dumb and unnecessary situations all the time, even if he had nothing to do with it, picture it: Evan at the grocery store. He reaches for a tomato, and suddenly, the entire produce section collapses. Why? No clue. It’s like the universe whispers, “Evan, you’re too normal. Here’s chaos.”. He can also perform psychic attacks to blow up your pancakes because waffles are better.
