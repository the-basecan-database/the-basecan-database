'Daniel'

“I'm just too strong bro.”

Description: This man is on steroids! He is stupidly strong, able to lift buses with only one finge.! While he seems like that typical guy you meet around the corner of every street ever, he is anything but norma! Even though he is calm most of the time, the one moment that you screw up, he’ll crush you so hard off the face of the planet that people around to witness this event would think you simply disappeared, so don’t mess around with this guy. Although his weakness is seafood, no dead sea life should be within two meters of this man or he’ll die inside and you’ll be able to get some hits in, if your intentions are just that, giving you a chance to defeat him. Not that it’d make sense though, unless you’re that typical Disney Channel villain. He sometimes works at the EALMAER part-time, but don’t expect to see him that much. He is an alright person to be around.
