The LARGE Shift

POV: You wake up encased in a large land mass

Description: In prehistoric times, an odd teleportation occurred where the modern Basecan archipelago stands today. The archipelago had actually appeared off the coast of Cyantria, seemingly swapping positions with another unknown landmass thats is thought to be from another world many years ago with archaeology backing this up, with clear borders in the rock down to a depths of about 500 meters, where they stop. We still don't know where the old land was or where this archipelago came from. It would also explain oddities occurring on this island.
