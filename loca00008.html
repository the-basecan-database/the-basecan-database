Kömentari Islands

I think they're the both the same town.

Description: The Kömentari Islands, located north of the Paragara Archipelago and south of Faraway, comprising of a large island with a small cluster of islands. It is home to two towns, Jrugden and Danigunzal which are often confused for each other despite the fact that they are not the same towns just because they have similar beaches and buildings.

Like the archipelago to the south, it gets many people during the summer, but considerably less. It's a second choice for those who do not want a lot of people.
