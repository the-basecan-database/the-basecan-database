Basamba (island)

The main island.

Description: Located in the country of Basecan, it is the main island of Basecan, giving it a more normal attitude compared to other major islands in the country. The rivaling towns of Naveli and Nadial are the major hubs of the country with Crosscrescent, Sunny Hills and Portmanteau being smaller town.

Being the main island, it recieves many things in return. Capsione National Park being one, being a rich picturesque taiga forest in a forever winter. Forp Jrug is also a historic landmark, due to its role in many wars across Basecan's history. The Burning Tower is also here, which is self descriptive.

Basamba is one of the most valuable land masses in all the world, due to the abundant of natural resources and fertile ground, the island is covered with mines and farms. However, this causes almost every corporation in the world is trying to get their hands on any part of the island they can, however, the island’s residents (and the entire country as a matter of fact) aren’t all that thrilled about multi-trillion dollar corporations walking in and taking their land, just like what happened in Colambriea.
