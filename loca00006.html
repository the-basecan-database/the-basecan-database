Faraway (island)

Chicken sandwich central.

Description: Located in the country of Basecan, Faraway Island is the home to the The Cult of Chicken Sandwich. It also has many mannequins on the islands due to activity by the TCCS, piling up like landfills, creating a high mountain able to be visible from space. The TCCS base is located underground in the third largest cave system in the country. Other than these surface level tidbits, we don't know much else about Faraway due to the TCCS's defence system.
