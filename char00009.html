Ken Molar Fix It Hider

“Giving people nice smiles is my passion”

Description: You know who this particular fellow is. He will fix and repair your teeth to perfection and has social anxiety. His skill-set includes dentistry, Ken, and hiding behind his walls by staying at home. He is shorter than your mom (sometimes), weaker than Ken (and you, sometimes), and is not very baller.

He often allies with the Tooth Fairy (they collect teeth, he helps to collect them!) in his dentist building and always loses against them. He is, in someone else’s definition, a professional high end dentist and a very reserved man, just don’t look in your fathers’s encyclopedia to never find it!
