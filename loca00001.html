Colambreia (island)

Island of all time.

Description: Located in the country of Basecan, it houses the largest cities in the country, that being Laproc. It is also home to the towns of Bricomba, Ubor Daleed, Thunderstorm, Townsville Village and Stropshire. Jupyter Lake is also in this island, the only recognized lake in the country.

The Colambriean Railway, major railway on the island, connects Laproc to both Townsville Village and Bricomba. The Colambriean Nuclear Power Plant also located on the island, is the only source of major energy (not including the many hydroelectric dams and wind farms across the country.)
