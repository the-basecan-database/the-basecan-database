The Curse of “27”

WHY THE HECK IS 27 ALL OVER THIS GOD FORSAKEN COUNTRY?!

Description: Throughout the entire recorded history of the islands, some of the most important historical events had ended with a number of something being 27, an example of Forp Jrug was taken over by cultists in 1327, the cultists reinforcements were 27 minutes away when the raiders had captured the Fort in 1389. Another great example is during the siege of cape Colambrie in 1439, the siege lasted 27 days and 27 people died from starvation. Another example of the curse is during the revolution for independents in 1698, a peace treaty was signed 27 weeks(189 days) after the start of the revolution and the islands gained full independence 27 days later after the peace treaty was signed. The archipelago was discovered in the year 27.
