Sunny Hills

Welcome to Sunny Hills

Description: The small town sites on the south-east most point of Basamba. With a population of about 12 000, the town only has 1 major shopping center, 3 gas stations, and around 9 fast food restaurants. Sunny Hills is ranked as the most normal town in the country.
