Krabe (island)

The water that hits different.

Description: Located in the country of Basecan, the island is mostly just water treatment plants and factories. This island is responsible for the creation of the water that hits different at 3am. The only inhabited town on this island is the town of Benachora, with 4,684 permanent residents at all times, regardless of what happens there.
