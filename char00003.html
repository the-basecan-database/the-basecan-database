Frank the Tank

-=+ PROTECTOR OF DIAMOS +=-

Description: Frank the Tank is a bear with a tank roaming the island of Diamos. It is its protector and has warded everyone that has attempted to get on the island since 1931. It has developed a radar system to detect any intruder on the island.

In 1919, Frank was presumably born on the island in the Grand Diamos Forest. Many towns were industrializing on the island so in 1927, it found a tank being built on the island and took it over, purging the entire island. It cleared the island in 1930, three years later. Diamos has became a nature preserve ever since this incident. It replaced its tank in 1961 with a M48 Patton, being its primary tank ever since.
