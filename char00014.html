Malaylaymadargenmemadorgenajung-Penagontarentamanagentaniumerenegen

a.k.a. "Malaylay"

“I see a car, and then I don't.”

Description: Malaylaymadargenmemadorgenajung-Penagontarntamanagentaniumerenegen is someone to behold. While their name is quite something in and of itself, that is not what defines this guy. Their defining trait is their ability to summon explosions with the power of them ranging from grenades to nuclear explosions.

They don’t do much on their free time except for blowing up cars and scrap metal in the junkyard. They are quite temperamental too, often getting angry at the littlest things (and then promptly blowing them up.) They only calm down from said short bursts of rage upon consuming a 10 hour burst energy, although nobody knows how this works. While on the influence of the 10 hour burst energy, they will not be easily angered, though they are prone to summoning explosions at random, and they immediately fall asleep when they feel the crash right afterwards.

Attempts have been made to contain or arrest this individual, but all have failed due to the fact that they blow the wall off the building they are held in. They went missing around a week ago and nobody has seen them since.
